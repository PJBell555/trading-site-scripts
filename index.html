Martingale + Karpathy Loop

Risk recovery / 8/10 / in use

Reference

Four-step Martingale sizing tied to user equity and risk settings. The Karpathy loop studies closed trades and adjusts the advisory threshold only after enough evaluated outcomes exist.

Current profile default: trade only when advisory clears the learned threshold, stop exits immediately, reset after the fourth losing step.

Live trading / 8/10
